WHAT's NFIP?

National Field-visit Information Portal is an authenticated gateway to store and retrieve data on the field visits that are being conducted by MOHFW staffs all over the nation.With NFI, MOHFW officials can store and get back the reports of the field visits anywhere at any time.The main Goal of NFIP is to provide an simple IT platform to record and analyze field visits and at the
same time ease the work of government officials.

NFIP is not just designed for MOHFW but also for the use of public.People all over the nation can raise out their issues in NFIP and get in touch with MOHFW.
